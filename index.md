## About Me

Hi, I’m Mizuki Hikaru.

I keep two other websites:
<a href="https://peaceful.design">peaceful.design</a> for design work, and
<a href="https://mizuki.codes">mizuki.codes</a> for programming.

I’m not all that into hobbies, but I’ve spent a lot of time thinking, resting,
and wandering - both mentally and geographically. I grew up in Melbourne and
have lived in places like Silicon Valley, Sydney, Tokyo, Berlin, and even Cusco
for a while. I’ve moved around a lot, sometimes chasing work, sometimes just
chasing a better social life, sometimes just needing change.

I like music with a story - lately I’ve been listening to a lot of Randy
Newman. I used to play piano when I was younger. These days I mostly enjoy a
quiet life: long stretches in bed, a cup of coffee or tea depending on the
mood, and the calm of not needing to be anywhere.

I was raised Catholic. I’m somewhat religious now, though I wasn’t always. I
went through about a decade as an atheist before finding faith again. I’m
always trying to live a good life. It’s something I’m constantly learning about
and growing into.
